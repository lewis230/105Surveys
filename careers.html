Working at 105 Surveys

A Place to Build Your Career

At 105 Surveys, we hold ourselves to a high standard — on every project, for every client. The work we deliver sits at the critical early stages of the design and planning process, and we treat it that way. That expectation runs through everything we do.

We invest in our people and in how we work. Our surveyors have access to the latest laser scanning and CAD technology, clear processes, and the autonomy to manage projects from start to finish. We're always looking to improve, and we expect the people who join us to bring that same mindset.

We're based in Liverpool, with projects across the North West — spanning residential conversions, commercial developments, heritage buildings, and large-scale infrastructure. The work is varied, the clients are demanding, and the standard is consistently high.

  • Varied project types across the North West
  • Latest laser scanning and CAD technology
  • Clear processes and full project autonomy
  • High standards, demanding clients, rewarding work
