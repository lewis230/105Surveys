About Us

Accurate Survey Data.
Delivered on Time.

105 Surveys provides measured building, topographical, utility, and setting out surveys across the North West — trusted by architects, developers, contractors, landscape architects, and planning consultants.

Our Approach

Built on Accuracy and Trust

At 105 Surveys, we deliver measured building, topographical, utility, and setting out surveys across the North West. Every project is managed closely to ensure the data you receive is accurate, clearly presented, and delivered on time.

We understand that inaccurate or late survey data causes delays, design revisions, and unnecessary costs. That's why we use advanced laser scanning equipment and hold ourselves to the standard our clients expect.

Supporting projects from small residential conversions through to large-scale commercial developments, we take pride in clear communication, well-structured drawings, and fast turnarounds.

  • Qualified surveyors on every project
  • Advanced laser scanning technology
  • Clear, well-structured CAD drawings
  • Based in Liverpool, covering the North West
