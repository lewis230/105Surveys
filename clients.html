Case study

The architects and heritage consultants needed accurate existing drawings before design could begin on a Grade II listed Georgian property.

We carried out a high resolution laser scanning survey covering internal floor plans, external elevations, roof plan, sections, wall thicknesses, door and window positions, ground levels and topographical data. Everything the design team needed to work from with confidence.
